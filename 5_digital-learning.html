Digital-Learning

Introduction

Digital learning is the use of technology to support education and improve the learning experience. ICT tools such as computers, tablets, projectors, and online platforms make lessons more interactive and engaging for students..

Online Learning Platforms and Tools

Students can use digital platforms like Google Classroom, YouTube, and educational apps to learn new topics and complete activities. These tools allow students to study both inside and outside the classroom.

Smart Classrooms and Modern Education

Smart classrooms use technology such as digital screens, online presentations, and internet resources to improve teaching. ICT helps teachers explain lessons more clearly and makes classes more interesting for students.

Benefits of Digital Learing for Students

Digital learning helps students understand lessons faster through videos, animations, and interactive activities. It also increases student participation and motivation. Students can learn at their own pace and review lessons anytime.

Challenges of ICT Access in Local Schools

Many local schools face problems such as limited internet access, lack of digital devices, and insufficient ICT training. These challenges make it difficult for students to fully experience digital learning.

Flexible Learning Anytime and Anywhere

ICT allows students to continue learning outside school hours. Students can access lessons, assignments, and educational materials from home or other places. This makes learning more flexible and convenient.
