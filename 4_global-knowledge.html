Access to Global Knowledge

Introduction

Access to global knowledge means students can learn from information around the world through ICT. Using the internet and digital technology, students can explore educational websites, online lessons, videos, and articles beyond their textbooks. This gives students more opportunities to improve their learning and understand different ideas and cultures.

Internet and Worldwide Learning

The internet allows students to connect with knowledge from many countries. They can learn about science, technology, languages, and world events in real time. ICT helps students discover new information quickly and makes learning more open and connected.

Educational Websites and Online Resources

Students can use online resources such as educational videos, e-books, digital libraries, and learning platforms. Websites and apps help students practice subjects, improve skills, and study independently. These resources make learning more flexible and interesting.

Digital Divide in Myanmar schools

Many students in Myanmar still face challenges because they do not have reliable internet access or digital devices. Some schools also lack ICT equipment and trained teachers. This creates a digital divide where some students have more learning opportunities than others.

Benefits of Global Learing Opportunities

Access to global knowledge helps students improve critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills. Students become more independent learners and gain a better understanding of the world. It also prepares them for future education and careers.p>

ICT and Equal Access to Education

ICT can help reduce educational inequality by giving more students access to information and learning tools. When schools improve internet access and technology, students can receive better educational opportunities regardless of their location.
